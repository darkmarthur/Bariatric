I am a candidate?

EVERY PERSON WITH IMC HIGHER THAN 40 BETWEEN 17 AND 65 YEARS OF AGE.

EVERY PERSON WITH IMC HIGHER THAN 40 AMONG 17 AND 65 YEARS OF AGE. PEOPLE BETWEEN 35 AND 40 BMI WITH ANY OF THE FOLLOWING DISEASES:

  • Hypertension
  • Type II diabetes
  • Heart disease
  • Sleep Apnea Syndrome
  • Dyslipidemia (hypertriglyceridemia and / or hypercholesterolemia).
  • Cardiovascular diseases
  • Disabling arthritis and osteoarticular pain due to overweight.
  • Syndrome of respiratory insufficiency associated with obes
  • Infertility
  • Severe psycho-social problems and depression.
  • Repeated failures to lose weight

Patients with bmi of 30 - 35 kg / m2 and associated co-MORBILITIES that do not achieve a sustainable and durable weight loss, without improving their associated diseases with non-SURGICAL treatments.

Calculate BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) Class
• Less than 18 Under weight. Necessary to evaluate the signs of malnutrition.
• 18 to 24.9 Normal
• 25 to 29.9 Over weight
• 30 to 34.5 Over weight Degree of obesity I
• 35 to 39.9 Obesity grade II or severe. Relative high risk to develop cardiovascular diseases
• 40 to 49.9 Obesity grade III or Morbid Extremely high relative risk for the development of cardiovascular diseases
• Greater to 50 Extreme risk, go to Dr. Arturo Valdez.
