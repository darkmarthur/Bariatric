Frequently asked questions


Can I have an operation if I already have or had previous obesity surgery?

Of course, most of the patients have undergone previous procedures that did not have good results, which can be converted or re-operated for greater benefit

Do the surgeries hurt and are the wounds big?

These procedures are performed laparoscopically (with camera and special instruments), which causes minimal discomfort and only 5 small wounds smaller than 1cm are performed

Do I have to ask for a special permit in my work? and how much time do I need disability?

Usually it is only two days of hospitalization, returning to normal activities as soon as the hospital is abandoned. If possible or necessary, a permit of 3-5 days can be requested for a quieter recovery.

Is this surgery paid for by insurance?

In special cases and with adequate medical insurance, some insurers cover part of the procedure. This is discussed in the office

Is there any payment facility?

Yes, for hospital expenses there are facilities with some credit cards (payment for months without interest)

How quickly can I operate?

If a patient is a candidate and does not have important diseases that have to be controlled before surgery, after their initial assessments can be operated two weeks later. A special diet is required before the surgery that has said time as duration.

Is there a "Super Food" to add to my diet after my surgery? (SuperFoods)

Sure, spirulina, since it is a source of protein of high biological value.

How many calories per day should a patient with a Manga or Bagua consume to improve their health and lose weight?

600-800 cal. from 0 to 6 months
800-1000 cal. From 6 months to 1 year
1000-1200 cal. from 1 year and up

How long does it take to have plastic surgery?

18 months

Is it true that you should not eat raw vegetables and fruits after surgery?

Preferably not, because of its high fiber content (removing the skin from fruits) and vegetables cooked and disinfected during the first months to avoid infection.
